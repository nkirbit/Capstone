Predictions

My results can define a function between COVID-19 case rates and interst in a particular business. Given a potential path of COVID-19, my model predicts how interests would shift. You can choose which path to look at with the toggle to the upper left on the graphs. Shift clicking will allow you to examine multiple paths at the same time.

I have generated five possible paths below:

  • A constant path
  • A path where the rate of COVID-19 cases change at a rate equal to the most recent change
  • A path where the rate of COVID-19 cases change at a rate equal to the average change over the past seven days
  • A random walk with no drift
  • A random walk with drift equal to the average change over the past 60 days.

Additionally, you can generate your own paths by filling out the form below the graph.
